Rules

  • You throw the dice.
  • You choose if you think the next throw will be higher or lower than your last throw.
  • Choose how many coins you want to use, the more you use the more is on the line!
  • Did you get it right? You double your stake, if you get it wrong you lose all of the coins, so be careful!
  • You are game over if you lose all of your coins, you win if you get to 100 coins.
  • You can put a max of 10 coins for each throw, and a minimum of 1 coin.
  • You start with 5 coins
  • You get a pop-up showing you if you won or lost
